Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Quelpit ("we," "us," "our") operates an on-demand platform connecting customers with independent service providers ("helpers") for moving, cleaning, furniture assembly, snow removal, and general household tasks. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

This policy applies to customers, helpers, and visitors to quelpit.ca. We comply with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Quelpit:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and service address
  • Booking information — service requested, date, time, duration, and special instructions
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe; we do not store full card details
  • Helper application information — work authorization status, availability, services offered, and background check results
  • Reviews and feedback — ratings and written comments submitted after a completed booking
  • Communications — messages sent to us via email or our booking and review forms

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and confirm bookings between customers and helpers
  • Calculate accurate pricing and generate secure payment links
  • Match customers with available helpers based on location and service type
  • Conduct background checks on prospective helpers
  • Publish verified reviews to helper profiles
  • Communicate booking confirmations, updates, and support responses
  • Improve our services and resolve disputes
  • Comply with legal and tax obligations under Canadian law

Who We Share Information With

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate the platform:

  • Helpers — receive your service address and booking details only after payment is confirmed
  • Stripe — our payment processor, to process transactions securely
  • Background check providers — to verify helper eligibility before onboarding
  • Legal authorities — if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users

Data Retention

We retain booking and payment records for as long as required under Canadian tax law, generally a minimum of 6 years. Helper records, including background check results and pay history, are retained for at least 3 years as required under the Ontario Digital Platform Workers' Rights Act. You may request deletion of other personal information by contacting us, subject to legal retention requirements.

Your Rights

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of information we are not legally required to retain
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Payment information is processed by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor — Quelpit never stores your full card details. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use quelpit.ca. We do not use this data to build advertising profiles or sell information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Quelpit is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "last updated" date at the top of this page.
