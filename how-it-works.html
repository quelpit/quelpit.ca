Our policies

Clear rules.
Fair for everyone.

We believe in being transparent about how everything works — for customers and helpers alike.

Cancellation policy — customers
Cancellation — No payment is taken at booking so there is nothing to refund if you cancel. Simply email support@quelpit.ca and your booking is cancelled at no cost, anytime before your helper arrives.
Rescheduling — Free anytime. If rescheduling within 6 hours of your booking, a 10% fee applies to your booking subtotal.
Helper no-show — You are never charged. Payment is only collected when your helper arrives at your door. One free reschedule always offered.
Processing fees — Applied only at the time of payment when your helper arrives. No processing fee is charged for cancellations before payment.
To cancel or reschedule — Email support@quelpit.ca anytime before your booking starts.
Cancellation policy — helpers
24+ hours before — No penalty. Plans change, and that's fine.
6–24 hours before — Up to 3 per calendar month at no cost. Beyond that, you'll be deprioritized in job matching for 30 days, meaning helpers in good standing are matched first.
Under 6 hours — Up to 1 per calendar month at no cost. Beyond that, you'll be deprioritized in job matching for 30 days and receive a written warning on file.
No show — Immediate suspension pending review, plus a written warning. Three no-shows within any rolling 90-day period results in a 1-month suspension from the Platform. Upon return, you'll start deprioritized in job matching — completing bookings reliably during this period restores your standing over time.
Why this matters — When you cancel a booking, the customer is notified immediately and offered a reschedule or refund. Repeated cancellations affect customer trust and your standing on the platform. Your reliability is your reputation — it shows in your reviews and your access to bookings.
Payment policy
Customers — No payment required at booking. Payment is collected securely via Stripe when your helper arrives at your location and before work begins.
Helpers — Paid every Friday for the previous week's completed bookings. No bookings completed that week means no payment that Friday.
Tips — 100% of any tip goes directly to the helper. Always.
Minimum — 2 hour minimum on all bookings
Dispute policy
Report window — Any issue must be reported within 24 hours of booking completion.
Resolution — We review both sides and resolve within 10 business days. Fair for everyone.
Damage claims — Covered by booking insurance (coming soon).
Appeals — One appeal allowed per dispute. We take every case seriously.
Common questions

Frequently asked.

How quickly can I get a helper?
We confirm most bookings within 2 hours. For same-day bookings availability depends on helpers in your area. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for guaranteed availability.
Do helpers bring their own supplies and equipment?
Quelpit provides helpers only. Customers are responsible for supplying their own cleaning products, equipment, and materials where needed. If you need cleaning supplies we can point you to a recommended kit.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
Report any issue within 24 hours of your booking completing. We review both sides and resolve within 10 business days. Our goal is always a fair outcome for everyone involved.
Can I request the same helper again?
Yes — if you had a great experience with a specific helper you can request them for future bookings. Subject to their availability in your area.
Are helpers insured?
Protection insurance on both the sides are coming soon.
How do I become a helper?
Fill out the helper application form. We review within 24 hours and arrange a background check. 🎉 Launch promo — background check covered by Quelpit for the first 10 helpers who join. Apply now to secure your spot before the promo ends. Once cleared you can start accepting bookings near your area.
Do I need a car to be a helper?
No. Quelpit is built for helpers who use public transit. We match you to jobs close to where you already are so you never need a car to earn on our platform.
When do helpers get paid?
Payments are sent every Friday for the previous week's completed bookings. For example — if you complete bookings between Monday and Sunday, your payment is sent the following Friday. If you did not complete any bookings the previous week, no payment is issued that Friday.
